Vibooding

The Face-Matching Puzzle Game

How to Play

Objective: Clear the board by matching 3 or more of the same faces!

Controls:

  • Use the mouse to aim the launcher
  • Click to shoot a face
  • Press SPACE to swap between the current and next face

Game Rules:

  • Faces stick to other faces or the ceiling
  • When 3 or more of the same faces connect, they pop
  • Any faces that become detached will fall
  • If faces reach the bottom line, the game ends

Scoring:

  • 10 points for each face removed
  • Bonus points for chain reactions
  • Clear the board for a massive bonus!
